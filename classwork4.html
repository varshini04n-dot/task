Official Notice

Our school Annual Day starts on 23 July. There are many competitions — everyone must participate in every event.

The Cultural Club is organising a one-day educational tour to the Science City on 20 July 2026 for Class 9 students. The bus will leave from the gate at 8:00 a.m. The fee is ₹500 per student (inclusive of travel and meals). Submit signed parent consent forms and the fee to your class teacher by 10 July 2026.

Annual Cultural Fest 2026

All students are hereby informed that the Annual Cultural Fest 2026 will be conducted from 20 July 2026 to 22 July 2026 in the school auditorium. Students are encouraged to actively participate in events such as singing, dancing, drama, quiz competitions, drawing, painting, essay writing, and group performances.

Interested students should register with their class teachers on or before 10 July 2026. Practice sessions begin 12 July 2026 after regular class hours. Winners will be awarded certificates and prizes during the closing ceremony.

Library Rules and Regulations

The school library will remain open from 8:30 AM to 4:30 PM on all working days. Students may borrow a maximum of two books for fourteen days. A fine will be charged for overdue books. Any damage or loss must be reported immediately to the librarian.
